FAQ & Rules

Placeholder answers · Final details confirmed before on-sale
How does the kayak-in, kayak-out thing work?

You can drive in like a normal person — but the spirit of the thing is the river. Kayak and canoe rentals run from Bone's Kayak & Campground, with a dedicated float window on Saturday from 9am to 4pm. Details on rentals and shuttles coming soon.

What should I bring?

Tent, sleeping setup, river shoes, sun protection, a costume or three, and a refillable water bottle. A full packing list will land here before the event.

What's the deal with food and water?

Food vendors will be on site all weekend, plus river brunch on Saturday and Sunday mornings. Potable water on the grounds. Vendor list coming soon.

Can I bring my own drinks? Glass?

Cans and plastic, yes. Glass, no — bare feet and dance floors don't mix with broken bottles. Full rules will be posted here.

Is River Dance 21+?

Age policy will be confirmed and posted here before tickets go on sale.

What about weather, cell service, and parking?

It's a river valley in Tennessee in August — plan for heat, pop-up rain, and spotty service. Parking is on site. Specifics coming with the official guest guide.
