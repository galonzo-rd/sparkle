What even is this?

Summer Camp For Disco People

Float the Buffalo River by day. Dance under the disco ball by night. Nightlife meets wildlife.

Float

Kayak or canoe straight from camp. Spend Saturday on the water with a few hundred new friends, then drift back to camp in time for the dance floor.

Camp

The campsite isn't just for sleeping anymore. Friday night brings summer camp games, an outdoor movie, and campsite DJs. Saturday, you camp out after a full day on the river and the big night on the dance floor.

Dance

The main event! River Dancers flock to the forest and bring the dance floor to life. Show off your moves, fits and vibes.
