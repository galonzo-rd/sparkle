What even is this?

Summer Camp For Disco People

Float the Buffalo River by day. Dance under the disco ball in the woods by night. Camp out, do it again. Kayak-in, kayak-out — a real dance party, dropped right in the middle of the woods and the river.

Float

Kayaks and canoes straight from camp. Spend Saturday on the water with a few hundred new friends, then drift back in time for the dance floor.

Camp

Two nights under the trees at Bone's Kayak & Campground. Tents, string lights, campfires, river brunch — pack in Friday, float out Sunday.

Dance

Disco balls, hazers, lasers, and a sound system deep in the woods. Two full nights of DJs spinning till late — for the first time ever.

Est. 2016 · Born on the river

Ten Years On The River

What started as one night of disco on a riverbank is now a decade-old tradition. 2026 is the big one — the 10th anniversary, and the first time River Dance runs two full nights.
